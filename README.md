## 🌊 Inspiration & Theme
**ShinkansenRush** is a high-speed, endless-runner racing game born from a love for Japanese bullet trains (Shinkansen) and a fascination with future transportation like the L0 Series Maglev technology. The game's entire aesthetic was hand-crafted for the love of trains:
* Beautiful "Ocean Blue", "Bright Cyan" and clear-water UI themes!
* Deep-sea blue train-station rooves, UI menus, and HUD displays!
* Custom-drawn procedural vectors of iconic Japanese rolling stock—from the elegant Hayabusa duck-bill to the famously elongated, flat aerodynamic nose of the mighty Maglev L0!
* Japanese toy-box visual styling with massive shiny buttons, floating emoji companions (🐻‍❄️ & 🐱), and huge bold interactive menus!

## 🎮 Game Mechanics
Jump into the conductor's seat of your favorite bullet train and race through the beautiful Japanese countryside! 

- **Choose Your Ride:** Before you depart from the main menu, tap the interactive train selector to choose exactly which custom train you want to drive! There are 6 custom models available (*Hayabusa, Doctor Yellow, N700, Komachi, Thomas, and the Maglev*), and the **Maglev** serves as the featured default on launch!
- **Dodge Oncoming Traffic!** **Swipe horizontally** along the tracks (or Use the `< >` Left/Right Arrow Keys) to switch lanes and dodge oncoming bullet trains flying in the other direction!
- **Zoom Past Landmarks:** As your train blasts down the continuous tracks, you will randomly whiz by iconic Japanese landmarks in the grassy edges, such as:
  * The Red & White **Tokyo Tower**
  * The towering gray **Skytree Tower**
  * A traditional **Don Quijote** superstore building!
  * The modern **Maglev Museum**
  * As well as named regional train stations like **Tokyo Sta**, **Osaka Sta**, and **Sapporo Sta** flying by!
- **Supersonic Boost:** **Swipe vertically** (or Press `Up`) to fire your afterburners and temporarily travel faster than the wind!
